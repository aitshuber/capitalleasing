What We Do
Capital Leasing Group (CLG) specializes in providing leasing solutions for Twister Trailer. Established in 2024, our mission is to deliver strategic financing options designed for individuals seeking high-quality, custom horse trailers. Whether you’re a national competitor or a weekend trail rider, we’re here to help you get your horses to their events with ease and style.

Our Focus
At CLG, we focus on creating leasing plans that align with your needs, whether you're looking for: Lower monthly payments, minimal upfront costs, tax benefits, hassle-free ownership with no resale concerns.  Leasing your next Twister Trailer through Capital Leasing Group ensures a seamless, cost-effective, and flexible financing experience.
